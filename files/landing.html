About Anthemy

Anthemy is a new and innovative way to engage with others who enjoy similar music. The website will try its best to pair you with someone, and you can chat about all the music that you love! You can also see all of your favorite statistics, like your top genres, songs, and artists, as well as a breakdown of all your playlists.
